Content Policy

Effective Date: June 6, 2025

1. Purpose

Our Content Policy ensures Activity Hub remains a safe, respectful, and legal place for students to share and discover activities.

2. Allowed Content

  • Original posts about campus clubs, events, and projects.
  • Thoughtful comments and feedback.
  • Educational resources and collaboration invitations.

3. Prohibited Content

  • Hate speech, harassment, or bullying.
  • Illegal activities or instructions for them.
  • Spam, unsolicited promotions, or phishing.
  • Explicit sexual content.

4. Copyright & Intellectual Property

Respect copyright. Do not post content you don’t own or have right to share. Submit takedown requests via our DMCA process.

5. Moderation & Enforcement

Content is reviewed by a combination of automated tools and human moderators. Violations may result in content removal, warnings, or account suspension.

6. Repeat Offenses

Users with multiple violations face escalating actions, up to permanent ban.

7. Reporting & Appeals

To report content, use the “Report” button on any post or comment. To appeal, email moderation@activityhub.io.

8. Safety & Privacy

Do not share personal information without consent. Protect privacy by anonymizing sensitive details.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We’ll post changes here with a new effective date. Continued use indicates acceptance.

10. Contact Information

Questions about this policy? Email us at privacy@activityhub.io.
