Our Mission

Activity Hub is building a new kind of campus ecosystem—one where student clubs, events, startups, and research projects are all discoverable in one place. We believe in giving students the tools to connect, create, and be seen.

Why We Built Activity Hub

On most campuses today, student activity is scattered across flyers, Instagram pages, outdated club directories, and word-of-mouth. There’s no central place to explore what's really happening across your university. We wanted to fix that.

Activity Hub started as a way to spotlight student-led innovation—whether that's an academic research project, a startup idea, or a cultural org hosting an open mic. It has since evolved into a full discovery platform, now being tested by real students and built with student input from across the country.

Our Journey

  • Fall 2023 – The Idea

    Frustrated with fragmented campus communication, the concept for Activity Hub was born: a unified place to find and share student-led work.

  • Spring 2024 – First Demo

    A prototype was built using open-source tools and AI-assisted coding. Students from several universities joined as early collaborators.

  • 2025 – Beta Launch

    Activity Hub enters private beta, with verified student orgs and research projects now onboarded from multiple schools.
