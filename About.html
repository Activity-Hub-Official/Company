Our Mission

We’re on a mission to unite every campus activity—clubs, events, research, and startups—into a single, searchable home.

Why We Built Activity Hub

Campus life today is fragmented: posters on bulletin boards, scattered social feeds, and outdated directories. Students miss out on countless opportunities simply because they don’t know they exist.

Activity Hub began as a prototype in Spring 2024, built with open-source tools and student feedback. Now, in private beta, we connect thousands of students to clubs, events, and collaborative projects every day.

Our Journey

  • Fall 2023

    Idea formed to centralize campus activities.

  • Spring 2024

    First working demo launched with early collaborators.

  • 2025

    Private beta begins at multiple universities.
