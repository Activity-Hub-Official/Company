1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Data: Name, email, password, profile details.

We do not sell personal data. We may share information with:

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to recognize your browser, remember preferences, and personalize content. You may disable cookies via your browser settings, but service functionality may be impacted.

5. Your Rights

  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request erasure of your account and personal data.
  • Objection: Opt out of marketing communications at any time.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system is fully secure—use unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. Contact us to remove any inadvertent submissions.

8. International Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in the United States or other countries. We ensure adequate protections such as standard contractual clauses where required.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We’ll post changes here with a new effective date. Continued use after updates indicates acceptance.

10. Contact Information

For privacy inquiries or to exercise your rights, email us at privacy@activityhub.io.
