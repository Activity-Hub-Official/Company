Our Partners

Activity Hub collaborates with visionary student organizations and university offices who believe in empowering campus communities. Here are just a few of our early supporters.

Consult Your Community (CYC)

A pro-bono student consulting club dedicated to helping local nonprofits and startups thrive through community-focused strategy and research.

Venture & Innovation Center (VIC)

St. John’s University’s hub for entrepreneurship, the VIC helps student founders turn ideas into ventures through mentorship, resources, and events.

ACM (CS & Cybersecurity Club)

The Association for Computing Machinery chapter at St. John’s, focused on coding, cybersecurity, and tech-driven student innovation.

Interested in Partnering?

We’re actively looking for campus clubs, incubators, and innovation centers who want to help students shine. If you’d like to collaborate or list your org on Activity Hub, we’d love to connect.
