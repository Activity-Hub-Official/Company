Why Activity Hub?

Activity Hub is the first student engagement platform that highlights not just clubs — but also startups, passion projects, and campus-led initiatives. Unlike most platforms that only focus on student organizations, we give all student leaders a space to share what they’re building and make it easier for others to find and support them. The result? A more vibrant, connected, and entrepreneurial campus community.
