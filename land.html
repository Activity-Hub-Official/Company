Why Activity Hub?

Activity Hub isn’t just another club directory—it’s the first engagement platform that shows what’s really happening on campus. Clubs, startups, side projects, events—it’s all here. We make it easy for people to find what’s going on and get involved. The result? A campus that feels more connected, more creative, and more like a community.

Features

Post

Are you a student leader with a club, startup, or side project? Share it on Activity Hub in just a few steps.

Explore

Browse dozens of student-run clubs, businesses, and projects, with all socials in one spot, so you never have to dig around.

Verified Listings

See a blue badge? That means the listing is officially recognized by your university. Others are independent or working toward recognition.

Stay Notified

Follow your favorite clubs, businesses, or projects and get notified when they post updates or host events.

Built with purpose

Activity Hub is a student-led initiative created to fix a real gap in campus life. Most platforms only list officially approved clubs — and nothing else. We built Activity Hub to highlight everything students are working on: businesses, side projects, and creative work that often get overlooked. Development is supported by grants and donations from people like you who believe in giving students space to be seen, supported, and connected.
