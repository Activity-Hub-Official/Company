Why Activity Hub?

Overview

Activity Hub brings everything you need to get involved on campus into one place. Whether you’re looking to join new clubs, discover upcoming events, or collaborate on research projects, Activity Hub simplifies your campus experience.

With personalized recommendations, real-time updates, and seamless peer collaboration, staying connected and engaged has never been easier.

Student‑Focused Features

Discover Organizations

Browse and join clubs that match your interests.

Events & Reminders

RSVP and get automatic reminders.

Personalized Feed

See activities tailored to you.

Peer Collaboration

Chat, form study groups, and collaborate.

How Students Use Activity Hub

Sign In & Customize

Log in with your campus email and set your interests.

Discover & Join

Browse clubs, events, and research groups that excite you.

Engage & Connect

RSVP, chat with peers, and build your campus network.

Built with Purpose

Built by students for students, Activity Hub is completely open source—designed to evolve with the needs of campus communities.

Our ongoing development is supported by grants and generous donations, ensuring that Activity Hub remains free, flexible, and future‑ready.
